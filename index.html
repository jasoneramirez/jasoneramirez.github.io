About

I am a postdoctoral researcher working at the intersection of mathematical optimization, machine learning, and interpretability. My work focuses on developing principled models that balance performance, transparency, and decision support.

Research

My research explores optimization-based approaches to interpretable machine learning, with applications to decision-making systems and scientific modeling.

Teaching

I have teaching experience in mathematics, optimization, and data science, at both undergraduate and graduate levels.
